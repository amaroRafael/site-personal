Antecipado -

The Antecipado is a platform that can be accessed through a smartphone or tablet with Android or iOS, or through a computer. It works as a quick registration. You register your information on anticipated app, which will generate a qr-code. Ready! Now everywhere who accept Antecipado, you no longer have to register. just show the qr-code to the attendant, who will read with the scanner, and you will already be able to participate in the promotion, enter the ballad or up to check in at the hotel.

To make it even easier, you can also participate in promotions directly from the app. Just access the "Campaigns" tab and choose which you want to participate.

Crmall -

The CRMALL is a system that has as main objectives the training and management of a customer database, with consequent actions of marketing and CRM in this data. The CRMALL enables the creation, management and segmentation of customer bases. This mass of data is not a homogeneous group, ie the purchasing decisions of a 30-year-old mother are different from those of a young teenager. Based on this knowledge, CRM and marketing strategies are drawn compatible with the target audience. The system enables to make several market analysis: target audience, customer flow, buying behavior, etc.
